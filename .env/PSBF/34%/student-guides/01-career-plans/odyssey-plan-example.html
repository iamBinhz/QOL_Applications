My Career Plan

After I graduate from university I want to get a good job in banking or finance. I think banking is a good career because it pays well and has good career progression. I want to work at a big bank like Vietcombank or HSBC.

In 1 year: I will study hard and try to get good grades. I will also try to find an internship somewhere.

In 2-3 years: I will graduate and start working. I hope to get a job at a bank. I will work hard to get promoted.

In 5 years: I want to be a manager or senior person at a bank. I want to earn a good salary and have a good life.

Plan B: If I can't work at a big bank, I will work at a smaller bank instead. Or maybe I will work in a finance company.

✗ "Get a good job" — what job? What type of banking? What role?
✗ "Try to find an internship somewhere" — where? How? When?
✗ "Work hard to get promoted" — this is not a plan with concrete actions
✗ Plan B = "smaller bank" — NOT a genuinely different direction
✗ No visual elements — just text paragraphs on a page
✗ No dashboard, no questions, no milestones, no self-awareness
✗ No connection to values — why banking? What about this person?
✗ Could have been written by AI — nothing authentic or specific
