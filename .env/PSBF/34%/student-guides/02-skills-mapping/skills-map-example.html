This gap analysis is what separates 50% from 70%+. A student who lists capabilities without analysis scores a bare pass. A student who identifies their biggest gaps, explains what they mean, and connects them to specific milestones in their Odyssey Plan demonstrates the "explicit connections between skills, growth, and future pathways" required for outstanding marks.
